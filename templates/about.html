ABOUT RESEARCH

Introduction to swamp pine

   As a tree species introduced in the early 1930s of last century, the pine tree grows rapidly and ADAPTS well, which is an ideal tree species for afforestation. The research on the breeding of swamp pine mainly focuses on the characteristics of turpentine, seed orchard construction, genetic determination and so on.

  The shooting rhythm is also one of the important physiological characters of swamp pine. The research on the shooting count of swamp pine is still blank at present, which is an important breeding index.

Website introduction

  This website is based on deep learning algorithm development, programming through Python language, automatic counting of swamp pine.

Some studies have proved that the average number of shoot extraction can be used to investigate the relationship between young and adult growth rate, and is expected to be an early predictor. The study on the number of shoot extraction of introduced species is an important way to understand their growth characteristics and adaptability. This website can automatically count the number of swamp pine shoots, which will bring great convenience to users.

At present, there are few researches on the shoot counting of swamp pine. in China, so we mainly applied it to the construction of the shoot counting model of swamp pine, to realize the automatic calculation of the number of shoot shooting of swamp pine, so as to realize the precise and efficient shoot counting of swamp pine., and to provide the technical support of intelligent seed examination for swamp pine breeders.
